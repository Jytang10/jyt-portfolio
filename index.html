About Me

James Tang, Front End Developer

I'm James Tang, a full-stack web developer with a passion for coding and learning. After teaching abroad for the past 3 years, I realized I wanted to find an outlet to use technology to positively increase efficiency in our daily lives. I have always had a love of studying new languages, and I quickly found that programming was the perfect path towards challenging my learning and productive output.


Completing the accelerated web development program at LearningFuze trained my analytical problem solving skills while equipping me with a robust knowledge of Javascript, React, CSS and many more technologies. Creating a variety of dynamic, interactive projects affirmed my decision to pursue a life long career in programming and web development.


Now, I am seeking opportunities to continue learning while challenging myself in the development field. I am eager to apply my skills and take on projects which will bring value and efficiency into our daily lives.

The Peanut Gallery

A Peanuts themed card matching game based on "Memory Match". I used JavaScript and jQuery to render elements dynamically and to program game functionality while using HTML5, CSS3 and Bootstrap 4 for design, display and responsiveness.

Record Repo

An online vinyl sales shopping cart application. Users can view, add, update and delete items from their shopping cart before placing their final order. ReactJS, JavaScript and JSX were used to design the front end portion of the application while the full LAMP stack was utilized (MySQL database called using PHP scripts) to handle the application's data.

Photo Pilgrim

A final capstone team project where I had the roles of Team Lead & Front End Lead. Photo Pilgrim is a photo sharing and discovery social media application. I primarily utilized ReactJS, JavaScript and JSX in order to create the majority of the components, and later added React Routing.
